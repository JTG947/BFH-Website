About Us

The primary objective of BFH is to mitigate the far-reaching consequences of global challenges, including armed conflict, displacement, pandemics, artificial intelligence, climate change, and exploitative corporate practices, thereby promoting a more resilient and equitable society that benefits humanity as a whole.

We Are BFH

The mission of BFH is centered on fostering a more harmonious and prosperous world, guided by the principle of "Live and Let Live." In pursuit of this objective, the corporation will prioritize philanthropy, dedicating a substantial portion of its profits to a dedicated fund that will be managed in accordance with a set of guidelines designed to maximize the positive impact on humanity. This approach seeks to create a sustainable and equitable society, where the well-being of all individuals and communities is prioritized.

BFH's mission is to channel its resources towards addressing pressing global issues, such as humanitarian crises, corporate exploitation, and sustainable development challenges. Specifically, the corporation will allocate funds to support relief efforts in regions affected by conflict, such as Ukraine, by providing essential aid and alternative solutions to military weapons. Additionally, BFH will engage in advocacy and initiatives to counter the harmful practices of corporations that prioritize profit over human well-being, by promoting transparency and responsible business practices. Furthermore, the corporation will invest in innovative agricultural projects in Africa to address the pressing issue of food insecurity, thereby empowering local communities to achieve sustainable food sovereignty. By tackling these critical issues, BFH aims to make a positive impact on humanity and promote a more equitable and just world.

"Benefit For Humanity" is a corporation with a noble mission: to foster a society devoid of destructive warfare and other calamitous events that endanger all life on Earth. Their focus extends to emerging threats such as artificial intelligence and human-induced climate change, which could exacerbate global warming and trigger catastrophic natural disasters. Moreover, the corporation recognizes the pernicious impact of harmful corporate practices, which not only perpetuate addiction but also impair human cognitive functions. In essence, "Benefit For Humanity" endeavors to mitigate these existential risks and promote the well-being of both present and future generations.
