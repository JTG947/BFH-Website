Our Services

Crypto Wallet

BFH provides a secure digital wallet where users can store various cryptocurrencies as it supports multiple cryptocurrencies storage all at same place. It offers features like two-factor authentication (2FA) and multi-signature security to ensure the safety of users' funds.

Exchange

BFH operates a cryptocurrency exchange platform where users can trade a variety of cryptocurrencies against each other or against fiat currencies. The exchange offers features such as spot trading, limit orders, and real-time market data to facilitate trading activities. Users can access the exchange via the web platform or mobile app, making it convenient to trade on the go.

Cross-Chain Bridge

Cross chain transfers or bridging assets from blockchains such as Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain (BSC), Cronos Bridge which further supports token Transfers from Polygon and Polkadot Ecosystem

Crypto Loans/Margin Trading

This is something that the exchange would launch in version 2 of the updated blockchain as Margin trading or Futures/Options trading requires approval from Financial Self regulatory Organization in Canada which the corporation is still in the process of getting.
